DEPARTMENT OF PLANNING AND ZONING
The city's planning and zoning administrators can access US Geographic Survey city names and associated data such as County, State, Latitude, and Longitude as made available via the Small Business Association (SBA). (See http://www.sba.gov/about-sba-services/7617 for more info.)

DEPARTMENT OF MOTOR VEHICLES
The city's DMV administrators, police officers, auto sales professionals, and repossessors can look-up vehicle make and model by the VSN (Vehicle Serial Number)...i.e., a fictitious identifier analogous to a VIN, or Vehicle Identification Number.
